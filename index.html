We are a group of programmers that are passionate about our craft. We aspire to the values of the Software Craftsmanship Manifesto.

Our meeting format differs from your usual technology user group. At a meeting we have:

  • 0-3 Lightning talks (up to but not over 5 minutes each) - Anybody can talk about a topic they feel will be of interest to the group.
  • ~30 min - Assigned Reading Discussion
  • ~60 min - Fellow Craftsman-run Coding Exercise
